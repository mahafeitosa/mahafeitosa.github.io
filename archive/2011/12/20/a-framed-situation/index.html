A framed situation

I went to the frame shop to frame the Skyrim map that came with the game. Getting there, I participate in the following conversation with the woman assisting me:

Woman: Oh nice map, what place is this?
Me: Skyrim
Woman: Where is it?
Me: Tamriel
Woman: Ah… Never heard of it… Have you ever been there?
Me: Ermm...I live there…
Woman: O.O … OK!
