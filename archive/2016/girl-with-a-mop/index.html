25 June 2016

Girl with a mop

It was a very sunny day in the old town of Corfu, Greece. We've been walking all morning exploring the narrow cobblestone streets, sipping on water and juice. One of the crossroads in this part of the city caught my attention and, before I could adjust my camera and position myself, this girl holding her brand new mop passed by. Without hesitating and almost in the middle of the tile design on the ground below her, she struck a pose and a smile. I clicked.
