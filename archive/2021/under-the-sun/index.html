Under the sun

Written by Dina

Our skin shines under the sun, ocean water licks our faces with the salty happiness. Time makes a new step: the other water takes salt away, happiness is still on the face. We love to be under the sun and we love to lay in the shadow. We forgot how to count minutes. We lost the perception of duality. Here and now. See, hear, feel, enjoy.
